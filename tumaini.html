SENIOR ACCOUNTANT
TUMAINI STAFFING & RECRUITMENT
Pinetown, Durban

The following software experience is required:

  • Xero
  • Simplepay
  • Caseware
  • Excel
  • Efiling
  • Easyfile
  • Sage online (for some clients)
  • Sage desktop (Pastel for some clients)

The job entails accounting and payroll services to a portfolio of clients of varying size and in various industries.
Experienceis required in the following, but not limited to:
  • Prepare Monthly Management accounts
  • Prepare Annual Financial Statements
  • Prepare Fixed Asset Register
  • Prepare VAT workings and submissions
  • Process payroll, EMP201 and EMP501 returns
  • Prepare ROE returns for Workmens Compensation
  • Prepare various reconciliations for the accounting workpaper file at year end
  • Prepare Tax computations for review
  • Data capturing in order to prepare clients monthly accounts
  • A thorough understanding of the accounting treatment and tax treatment of transactions and their implications for completing tax returns as well as annual financial statements
  • A person that has accounting and tax experience will be excellent
  • U-filing experience will be an advantage

BIDVEST PROTEA COIN

Unemployed youth who are interested in a career in the security industry are invited to apply for 12-months opportunities to be provided by Bidvest Potea Coin. The qualification will be National Certificate: General Security Practices (GSO) NQF Level 3 accredited by SASSETA (Safety Security Sector Education and Training Authority)

The requirements are as follows:

  • Age between 18 and 35.
  • Grade 10-12(matric)
  • Identity Document
  • Updated CV
  • Proof of residential address
  • SARS Document (Income ref number)
  • No Criminal record
  • Communicate Effectively in English
  • Be able to read and write
